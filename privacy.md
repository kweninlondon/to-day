# Privacy Policy for to-day

Last updated: 13 June 2026

to-day is a private to-do list app.

The app lets you create, edit, complete, archive, and delete your own tasks. Your task data is stored on your device and, when iCloud is available, synced using Apple iCloud/CloudKit so your tasks can appear on your own devices.

## Data

I do not sell your data.

I do not use advertising trackers.

I do not use third-party analytics.

I do not collect your task content on my own server.

I do not share your task content with third parties.

## iCloud sync

iCloud sync is provided by Apple and depends on your Apple ID, iCloud settings, device settings, and network connection.

Task data synced with iCloud is handled through Apple’s iCloud/CloudKit services.

## Support emails

If you contact support by email, I will receive the information you choose to send, such as your email address, message, device details, screenshots, or bug report information. I will only use this information to reply to you and help with your request.
