# Support for to-day

For support, feedback, or questions about to-day, email:

acreage_swoons_88@icloud.com

If you are reporting a bug, please include:

- Your device model
- Your iOS or iPadOS version
- The version of to-day you are using
- A short description of what happened
- Any steps that help reproduce the issue

to-day uses iCloud/CloudKit to sync tasks when available. Sync depends on your Apple ID, iCloud settings, device settings, and network connection.
