# To-day at a glance

To-day is a todo app that clears your mind. Inspired by the simplicity of organizing thoughts on paper, it helps you sort tasks into **today**, **this week**, **this month**, or **one day**.

Once it’s written down, it’s out of your head. So you can focus on doing, not remembering. Recurring tasks, ticking things off, it’s all designed to feel natural and simple. Write it down, and trust it’s there.

Its main tabs are:

- **to-day** for the Today, Yesterday, and Tomorrow views.
- **this week** for tasks you plan to do during the current week.
- **this month** for tasks you plan to do during the current month.
- **one day** for tasks without a near-term plan.
- **recurring** for tasks that repeat or reusable presets.
- **statistics** for completion summaries, graphs, and recurring-task progress.

Use the other pages in this guide to learn about tabs and views, task actions, groups, recurring tasks, statistics, settings, and recovery options.
