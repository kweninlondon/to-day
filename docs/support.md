# Support

## Bug reports

If something is not working as expected, please include:

- Your device model
- Your iOS or iPadOS version
- The version of to-day you are using
- What happened
- What you were doing just before it happened

## iCloud sync

To-day uses iCloud and CloudKit to sync tasks when available. Sync can depend on your Apple ID, iCloud settings, device settings, and network connection.
