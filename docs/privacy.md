# Privacy Policy

*Last updated: 13 June 2026*

**To-day** is designed to be a private todo app.

You can create, edit, complete, archive, and delete your own tasks.

**Your task data is stored on your device.**

When iCloud is available, to-day uses Apple iCloud and CloudKit to sync your tasks across your own devices.

## **Data**

To-day does not:

- Sell your data
- Use advertising trackers
- Use third-party analytics
- Store your task content on my own server
- Share your task content with third parties

## **iCloud sync**

iCloud sync is provided by Apple through iCloud and CloudKit. Sync depends on your Apple ID, iCloud settings, device settings, and network connection.

Task data synced through iCloud is handled by Apple’s iCloud and CloudKit services.

## **Support emails**

If you contact support by email, I will receive the information you choose to send. This may include your email address, message, device details, screenshots, or bug report information.

I will only use this information to reply to you and help with your request.
